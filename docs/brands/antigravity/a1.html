01 WHAT IT IS

A cinema camera. That happens to fly.

Most drones start with the airframe and mount a camera to the front. A1 was designed the opposite way: two large 1/1.8" sensors were chosen first, and then the smallest airframe the sensors would tolerate was engineered around them.

The result: full-hemisphere capture at 8K resolution — the entire upper half of the sphere recorded to a single frame — and a post-flight app that lets you pick your framing after the fact. A vertical for social, a horizontal for the edit, a panorama for the print.

The image pipeline, the stitching, and the color science were co-developed with the Insta360 X-series team. If you've ever looked at an Insta360 reframe and thought that's magic — this is the same magic, calibrated for the sky.
