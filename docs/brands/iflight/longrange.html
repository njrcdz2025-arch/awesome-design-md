FLY-SAFE · INDUSTRIAL FREQUENCY & AIRSPACE NOTICE
Longrange is a licensed act.

Every longrange and industrial iFlight platform ships with an ELRS 2.4 GHz encrypted control link and 5.8 GHz video downlink. In most jurisdictions, extended-range or beyond-visual-line-of-sight (BVLOS) operation requires an operator permit, a filed flight plan, and adherence to national frequency regulations. iFlight is a hardware manufacturer — the legal responsibility for compliant operation lives with the pilot.

Before every flight: check NOTAMs, verify local drone-flight zones (CAAC in China, FAA LAANC in the US, EASA in Europe, MLIT in Japan), confirm your transmitter is on a locally permitted frequency band, and never overfly non-consenting people. A 6 kg payload on a Taurus X8 has real kinetic energy. Fly it like it does.
