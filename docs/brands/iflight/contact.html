FLY-SAFE · THE UNBOXING PLEDGE
The hardware is ours. The airspace is shared.

Every iFlight platform, from the sub-260 g Alpha to the 6 kg-lifting Taurus X8, ships with a Fly-Safe checklist card. Read it once. Fly it every session.

Before every takeoff: Check the airspace map (B4UFLY / Drone Assist / DIPS / MLIT). Confirm you are not inside a temporary flight restriction. Do a pre-flight I/O check on flight controller battery voltage, ELRS link quality, GPS satellite count, and video signal strength. Never overfly non-consenting people or moving traffic.

In-flight: Maintain visual line of sight unless you hold a BVLOS waiver. Yield to manned aircraft — always. Yield to emergency services — always. If you lose signal, don't chase. Let return-to-home do its job.

After every flight: Log the flight. Inspect the airframe for fatigue cracks. Store LiPos at storage voltage. If something failed — even benignly — file a note. iFlight learns from your notes.

Longrange, industrial, and commercial operations require additional licensing in every major jurisdiction. iFlight is a hardware manufacturer. Legal responsibility for compliant operation lives with the pilot. Fly considered.

FAQ · WHAT PILOTS ASK

Frequently asked, plainly answered.

How long is the standard iFlight warranty? +
Twelve months from date of purchase on manufacturing defects. Motors, ESCs, and flight controllers are covered. Crash damage, propeller impact damage, and firmware-induced damage from third-party binaries are not covered. Register the serial number on shop.iflight.com within 30 days of purchase to activate warranty.
Do you ship internationally directly? +
shop.iflight.com ships to 42 countries directly with duties pre-cleared to the US, Canada, UK, EU (Munich hub), Japan, Singapore, Australia, and UAE. For every other destination, we route you to your nearest authorized dealer — they can beat direct shipping on both price and warranty coverage.
What's the difference between BNF and PNP? +
BNF (Bind-and-Fly) ships with a receiver installed — pick your radio protocol (ELRS 2.4 GHz, TBS Crossfire, DJI radio) at checkout, we bind at the factory. PNP (Plug-and-Play) ships without a receiver — you supply and install your own. Both ship without transmitter, batteries, or goggles.
Can I get a Fly-Safe briefing before my first flight? +
Yes. Every authorized dealer offers a free 30-minute Fly-Safe onboarding for first-time buyers — bring the airframe, we'll walk you through pre-flight, first-hover, and return-to-home muscle memory. Book via your dealer's contact page.
Do you sponsor race teams and content creators? +
Yes — Creator Program and Race Team applications are always open above. Approved creators receive hardware allocations, custom builds for signature projects, and priority RMA. Approved race pilots receive IFRC entry fee reimbursement, spare parts kits, and travel support for headliner events.
Where do I find firmware updates and tuning guides? +
shop.iflight.com/support hosts every firmware, every default Betaflight config, and every stock tune profile released since 2019. Signed builds only. Third-party binaries flashed on iFlight airframes void warranty.
