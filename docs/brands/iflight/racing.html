FLY SAFE · RACE-DAY RESPONSIBILITY
Race gates are boundaries. Spectator lines are the law.

Every IFRC event is stewarded by a Race Director whose call is final on airspace suspension. Drone weights, camera-hood angles, transmitter power and geofence configurations are all checked at pit-lane scrutineering. A DNS at scrutineering is faster than a DQ mid-race. Pilots are asked to respect fellow racers, spotters, and above all — spectators.
