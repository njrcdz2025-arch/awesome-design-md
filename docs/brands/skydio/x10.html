01 WHAT IT IS

Built for the missions that can't wait.

X10 flies in weather that grounds every other commercial drone. Its navigation stack fuses six 4K cameras with a purpose-built AI compute unit — no GPS, no cloud, no operator input required to keep it airborne.

The modular payload bay accepts three sensor stacks: VT300-L for long-range EO + thermal, VT300-Z for close-in high-zoom inspection, and NightSense for zero-lux tactical operations. Swap in under 30 seconds without tools.

NDAA-compliant, Blue UAS 2.0 listed, engineered in San Mateo, California, and assembled in the United States. Cleared for deployment by federal, state, and local agencies.
