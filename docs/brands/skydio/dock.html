01 WHAT IT IS

A drone that never leaves the site.

The Dock lives outside — on a rooftop, at a substation, above a construction gate — with an X10 already inside. When a mission triggers, the door opens, the drone lifts, the mission runs, and the aircraft returns and re-charges. Human input: zero.

Triggers come from three surfaces: scheduled (patrol every 6 hours), event-driven (a Genetec / Milestone camera alarm, a Skydio VMS rule), or manual (a duty officer clicks "launch" from mission control). The drone is airborne within 40 seconds.

One dock covers roughly 400 acres — a substation, a data-center campus, a rail yard, or a small municipality. Chain multiple docks together for continuous coverage of larger footprints.
